About CRUX

CRUX (Collaborative Research & University Exchange) is a dedicated platform for researchers, students, professors, and industry professionals to connect, share challenges, and find solutions in scientific research.

Our mission is to accelerate scientific discovery by fostering collaboration across disciplines and institutions. Whether you're stuck on a computational problem, seeking advice on experimental techniques, or looking for collaborators on groundbreaking research, CRUX provides the community and tools you need.

Key Features

  • Problem Sharing: Post research challenges and get expert advice from the global scientific community.
  • Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration: Connect with researchers from materials science, chemistry, biotech, computational fields, and more.
  • Knowledge Exchange: Learn from detailed discussions, shared methodologies, and practical solutions.
  • Visibility Controls: Choose who can see your posts - from public discussions to private collaborations.

Domains Supported

We support research across multiple domains including Materials Science, Computational/DFT, Chemistry, Biotech/Biology, Physics, Electronics, Environmental Science, and more.

Join CRUX today and be part of the collaborative research revolution!
